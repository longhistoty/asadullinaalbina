Born in Tyumen, Russia

Albina Asadullina, soprano

Graduated with honors from the Gnesin Russian Academy of Music in Moscow, specializing in classical vocals under the guidance of Lolita Semenina.
Participated in concerts organized by the Elena Obraztsova Foundation. Also, as part of Dmitry Korchak's musical project and the Obraztsova Foundation's "Opera Class," she performed in the opera-ballet "L'enfant et les sortilèges" by M. Ravel (roles of the Nightingale, Fire, and Princess).
Currently a scholarship holder of the Swiss Government Excellence Scholarship Program and a second-semester master's student in the class of Professor Malin Hartelius.
On November 22, 2022, she performed at the Geneva branch of the United Nations at a charity event aimed at supporting children and women in difficult life situations.
